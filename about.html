Our Services

Bath Geo-Environmental is an independent geo-environmental consultancy providing office & site based services for our client’s requirements, whether at feasibility, planning, design, or construction stage.

Our consultancy emphasizes the importance of early communication and active engagement with clients. This approach allows us to develop customized strategies for each project, ensuring clear objectives and a focused plan of action. By providing timely and relevant information, we empower our clients to make informed decisions. As an independent consultancy, we offer the flexibility and responsiveness needed to adapt to changing circumstances, delivering exceptional value and personalized service.

We are based in Bath and cover the whole of the UK, providing a range of services including geo-environmental services, Phase 1 desk studies reports, Phase 2 ground investigations and Factual & interpretative reports.

Sustainability

We are dedicated to offering environmentally conscious services, aiming to minimize our carbon footprint and reduce the embodied carbon in each project we undertake reducing the vehicle usage throughout our project involvement wherever possible. We consider the potential impacts of climate change in all our assessments and strive to provide engineering solutions. Our near-term objective is to become a carbon-neutral business.

Our Team

Our Managing Director, Rosa, is a Master Geology and Incorporated Engineer with over 14 years of professional geotechnical and geological experience in the UK as well as international experience, especially in research geology using 3D modelling and multiplicity of microscopies for petrologic studies. Site-wide UK experience comprised soil-rock testing, vertical and horizontal logging and geotechnical site supervision. Her expertise includes supervising and managing drilling, grouting, and nailing activities, as well as executing remedial works.

Rosa as part of previous Geo-Environmental Teams assisted delivering project cost control/pricing, managed the interface with clients and implemented project plan to ensure high-quality project delivery.

Rosa is a well-organised and highly competent and professional experience in the Construction Industry with strong experience in organisation and managing ground investigations.
